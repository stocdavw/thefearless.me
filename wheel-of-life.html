The Wheel of Life

The Wheel of Life is a 60-second excercise that gives you an astonishing view of your life satisfaction.

We have divided the human life experience into 7 areas; Psychology and emotions, Business and Career, Physical Health, Relationships, Finances, Growth and Contribution.

Below you will find a form which you can use for your self-evaluation. Each area of life has a brief description.

Score each area of life between 1-10, with 0 being "Couldn't be worse" and 10 being "Couldn't be better" The resulting image will be emailed to you, along with a report detailing your results and showing you what to do next.

Psychology and Emotions

Do you experience the emotions you want to experience on a daily basis? Do you feel it is possible for you to achieve your goals? Do you have reasons to strive for more? (1-10)

Business and Career

How do you feel about what you do for a living? Do you have a mission? Do you have a calling? Are you fulfilled? (1-10)

Physical Health

Not just how active you are, but how are your eyes, your bones, your teeth, your joints? Do you have any diseases? Have you had your annual checkups? (1-10)

Relationships

You may or may not have an intimate relationship. What about your children, your siblings, your parents, your close friends, acquaintances, and colleagues? (1-10)

Finances

Do you have a monthly budget? Do you have any assets? Do you know your net worth? How much do you need to spend? How much do you need to save? How much do you need to invest? How much do you need to give? (1-10)

Growth

Whether you are learning to play a musical instrument or you are taking college or university courses or you are reading a book, you are learning and learning is growth. You can experience growth in your Psychology and Emotions, in your Business and Career, in your Physical Health, in your Relationships, your Finances and in your ability to Contribute to others. (1-10)

Contribution

We can contribute through our own Growth, through our Finances, our Relationships, our Health, our Business or Career and our Psychology and Emotions. How much do you Contribute and how much would you like to Contribute? (1-10)
