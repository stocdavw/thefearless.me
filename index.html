    Follow the path to Alignment

    Follow these steps in order to fully develop into your true self.

    01

    Clarity

    In Clarity, we will find out what bothers you about your life, and what your "forgotten dreams" are. We work together with you to recreate visions and dreams and remove obstacles in order to achieve them.

    02

    Roots

    We approach your roots from a shamanic point of view. We let light fall on your dark spots and work on your connection to your ancestors and Mother Nature.

    03

    Shadows

    Shadows are parts of us that we have split off, oppressed or rejected. In Shadows, we will learn how all parts of our personality to be valuable.

    04

    Self-Love

    Now that we know who we truly are, we must learn to love our whole imperfect self. Self-love is so much more than a bubble bath or me-time. Self-love is like a muscle, you can train it.

    05

    Self Confidence

    Self Confidence is learned, and we will cover the theory as well as give you practical excercises. This is where you become "the FEARLESS me"

    06

    Joy

    When we rediscover who we really are, then we tap into the source of our Joy. Dive deep into the joy of life. Why? Because YOU are worth it. You are here in this world to experience joie de vivre.

    07

    Growth

    As the caterpillar becomes the butterfly, so too will you become someone new. It is inevitable. The only question is who do you WANT to become?

    08

    Passion

    If you burn for what you do, if you're passionate about it, you're willing to take risks, get up for it, make a fool of yourself for it, then you are truly alive. Let's find your Passion!

    09

    Stillness

    Stress has become socially acceptable. We are energized around the clock and no longer notice it. We have forgotten how to say "no". When was the last time you were really relaxed?

    10

    Abundance

    Abundance your birthright. Here you will learn the secrets of abundance. Many know them, but most do not live them. Let us show you how.

    11

    Spirituality

    The state of Awakening. When we recognize and understand what is happening around us. When we recognize the connections between thoughts, feelings and actions. When we completely trust our intuition. Then we are free.

    12

    Alignment
